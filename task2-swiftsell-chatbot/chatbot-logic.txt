
Chatbot Questions:
1. What's your name?
2. What's your email?
3. What's your phone number?
4. Are you a student or working professional?
5. Is your background tech or non-tech?
6. What career path are you interested in? (Full Stack, Data Science, etc.)
7. Are you ready to start within the next 30 days?

Conditional Messaging:
- Student + Tech → "You're eligible for our Full Stack Fast-Track!"
- Working + Non-Tech → "Many like you have transitioned successfully."
- Disinterest → "Thanks! You're always welcome to reach out later."

Qualified lead format (JSON):
{
  "name": "User Name",
  "email": "user@example.com",
  "phone": "9999999999",
  "background": "tech",
  "type": "student",
  "track": "Data Science"
}
